About

I'm Penny, a terrier mix. Here's a little about me! I was born on the streets of downtown Los Angeles, probably near skid row. I don't remember, exactly, but it was definitely an unsafe environment for a puppy. Luckily, a wonderful lady found us and took us in to her shelter.

We spent some time in her house, playing and sleeping, and eating some amazing puppy food. I felt really spoiled. My brother, Bronco, was the coolest out of all of us... He got a lot of attention from all the visitors to our rescue lady's storefront.

One day, this pretty lady came into the store with her friend. She was going to go for Bronco, but then her friend was all, "this one's way cuter." I felt so flattered. She came back the next day with her boyfriend, and they played with me for a whole hour! They went home to think it over, and then they came back the following day to take me home with them!

Ever since then, it's been smooth sailing. My human pets are the best ever... I just wish they took me to the park more often and led a more active lifestyle.
